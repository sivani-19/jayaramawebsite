Our Services

Residential Valuations

Involves assessing various factors such as the property's location, size, condition, amenities, and comparable sales in the area..

Commerical Valuations

Involves determining the market value of commercial properties, such as office buildings, retail spaces, industrial facilities, and other non-residential properties.

Industrial Valutaions

Typically include manufacturing facilities, warehouses, distribution centers, industrial parks, and other properties used for industrial purposes.

Leasehold Properties Valuations

determining the market value of properties that are subject to a lease agreement. the occupant or tenant holds a leasehold interest in the property,

Capital Gains Valuations

Capital gains tax is a tax imposed on the profit earned from the sale or disposal of a capital asset, such as stocks, real estate, or other investments

Valuation for Banking purposes

which can includes the income approach, sales comparison approach, and cost approach, among others.

Visa valuation

The value of a visa or immigration program.Visa valuations are carried out to determine the economic value and impact of specific visa programs or categories

Cost of construction Valution for IncomeTax

Determining the value of a property's construction or improvement costs. It is relevant for income tax purposes when calculating deductions or allowances related.

L&B Valuation Related Court

The valuation of land and buildings conducted in the context of legal disputes or litigation. To determine the market value or compensation amount associated with a property.

Valuation for liquidation purpose

Determining the value of assets or a company's assets during the liquidation process occurs when a company is unable to meet its financial obligations.
